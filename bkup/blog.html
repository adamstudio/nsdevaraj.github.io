BitCoin

Bit Coin - Bitcoin is an open source peer-to-peer electronic money and payment network Wiki!
Bit coins are mined by p2p network resources, till now 12 million bitcoins were mined. Source
The mining will continue until 21 million bitcoins.

Merchants have an incentive to accept the bitcoins because transaction fees are lower than the 2 to 3% typically imposed by credit card processors.
Speculators have been attracted to Bitcoin, fueling volatility and price swings.

Current value for bit coin is 1142 USD.
Just 2 Months back it was just 141 USD.

Wordpress and Baidu are some famous vendors accepting Bitcoins apart from contract killers.

The basis how it works is, as a user you will be given with unique id. Also, every coin have an unique id mapped to user owning it.
The whole information is stored across p2p network, including the transaction details.
